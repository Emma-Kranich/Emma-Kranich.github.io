About Me

Hi! I'm Emma Kranich, a sophomore electrical and computer engineering student at Cornell University. I am also planning to get a minor in biomedical engineering and pursue an early masters in biomedical engineering. I'm interested in the connection between biomedical engineering and electrical/computer engineering, specifically in relation to brain-machine interfaces. I would love to work for a company where engineering directly and positively affects humanity/society and any type of medical device company accomplishes this goal (brains just fascinate me). Outside of these, my other academic interests include neuroscience (clearly) and computer science. In my free time, I love to hike, beekeep, play piano, ski, and bake. I also love art and crime shows. As you can tell, I love to stay busy and rarely say no to any opportunity that comes my way. You can find out more about me by reading my resume or connecting with me on LinkedIn! Feel free to reach out via email as well!
